There are a million ways to build a
profitable product business.

but selling wholesale is our favorite!

Order volume is higher, quantities sold are larger and the customer lifetime value is greater when you sell wholesale.
Which means youre putting more money in your pocket!

But selling wholesale isnt easy peasy.

Selling wholesale requires different strategies than selling direct to consumer, offering custom products or even licensing your work. Wholesale requires you to consider buying seasons, product release cycles, fulfillment support as well as unique sales & marketing strategies. I know it sounds overwhelming.

But dont worry, you dont have to figure out wholesale on your own.

OUR COMMUNITY IS here to take the guess work out of selling wholesale and systematizing your wholesale business so that it is running with ease.

Im Katie Hunt, a business strategist & wholesale coach trusted by thousands of product brands!

Ive been where you are now. I started my product brand in 2008 (at the start of a recession) and blindly jumped into selling wholesale within 6 months, exhibiting at my first international trade show in 2009 as my launching point.

In 2011, I saw that my creative colleagues needed business support & could benefit from the knowledge I had amassed from 10+ years in the corporate world plus two MBAs in marketing & finance, not to mention my 8 years of scaling my own product brand so thats when Proof to Product was born.

With my guidance, my clients have

Surpassed $2M in annual revenue
10x wholesale revenue in a single month
Hit yearly sales goals by October, then took a month OFF!

I value simplicity in business. And, I have a knack for breaking down complicated strategies into simple steps so that you have stronger sales, higher profit & more support.

Whether youre brand new to business or youve been at it a while, our community will offer you support and strategies to help you reach your wholesale goals faster.
